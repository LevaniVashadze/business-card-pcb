It has a button, which activates 2 led lights and a small vibrating motor.

Silk screened is my website and the qr form of my website link.

# BOM:

- 3 resistors
- 2 LED lights
- 1 transistor
- 1 button
- 1 battery cell
- 1 mini motor disc
- 1 capacitor
